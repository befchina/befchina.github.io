The core of the BEF-China research platform consists of


i) Comparative Study Plots (CSPs)：27 comparative study plots (CSPs) of 0.09 ha each, of different species richness and successional stages, established in the Gutianshan National Nature Reserve (Zhejiang Province — details about the design are found in Bruelheide et al., Ecological Monographs 2011).

Fig. 1 Map of Comparative Study Plots (Figure B, D) (Bruelheide et al.2011)

Fig. 2 Landscape of Gutianshan National Nature Reserve

ii) The BEF-China Main Experiment: 566 experimental plots of 0.067 ha each, located at two experimental sites A and B in Xingangshan (Jiangxi Province), planted 42 native tree species and 18 shrub species differing in species richness and composition. More than 400,000 tree and shrub saplings were planted. (details about the design are found in Bruelheide et al., Methods in Ecology and Evolution, 2014).
