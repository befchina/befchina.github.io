Steering Committee

The platform is governed by the Steering Committee (SC) which is consists of representatives from both European and Chinese sides. Main missions are: 1) coordinating of running projects on the platform and final approval of new project applications; 2) developing the management mode of the platform and solving the important issues reported by the management group (MG); 3) supervising the implementation of the decision proposed by BEF-China members. The current term of SC is 2018.10-2023.10.

Management Group

The members of the Management Group (MG) are scientists from the Institute of Botany, CAS and Jingdezhen University. Main missions are: 1) implementing the decisions of SC; 2) being responsible for routine management of lab and field sites; 3) being responsible for financial management; 4) checking applications of membership, data and paper proposals; 5) collecting and technically checking project applications and forwarding it to SC for review; 6) organizing the SC annual meeting. The current term of MG is 2018.10-2023.10.
